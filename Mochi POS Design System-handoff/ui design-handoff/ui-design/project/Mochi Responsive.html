what adapts

The stage gauge survives every size — it's the anchor. Phone folds the day-rhythm + cart + co-host into a bottom-tabbed stack; new sale opens as a fullscreen sheet from the FAB. Tablet keeps the wall front-and-center with a 36px icon rail on the left and a 1-row cashier dock pinned to the bottom — perfect for thumb reach. Desktop spreads everything visible at once: rail, stage, ticker, wall, cashier, and the Mochi co-host bubble.
