09 · Seasonal mochi · calendar refresh

The mascot lives a year.

Mochi doesn't just sit there — she dresses for the calendar. Songkran flower crown in April, krathong float in November, red ribbon for Lunar NY. Each variant unlocks for 7–14 days. The app gets a fresh face six times a year, for free.

Santa hat. Snowfall layer behind the splash screen.
why it matters

A static mascot ages. A living mascot becomes a ritual — sellers and customers check in to see what Mochi is wearing this week. The variants are small SVG overlays on the base face, so the system stays cheap to maintain. Mark variant unlocks in the marketing calendar; each one earns a social post.
