🚀 LAUNCH: Pet Expo Championship · Jul 30 – Aug 2, 2026 · 5 sellers live

1 · The deadline drives everything

~9 weeks out. We plan backward from July 30, not feature-by-feature. Scope is 5 recruited sellers running their booths on MochiPOS live. The features are the easy part; recruiting, onboarding, connectivity, and support are what make-or-break a real pilot — so they run in parallel (Track B below), starting now.

4 · Two tracks, in parallel

Track A — Build (the 6 issues above). ~7 weeks, two of us + Claude, partitioned by lane. Claude reviews every code PR (/review) and keeps main green.
Track B — Pilot operations (starts now — the part pilots actually fail on):
  • ☐ Recruit 5 sellers — a sales pipeline; weeks of lead time. Start this week.
  • ☐ Onboarding + 1-page quick-start — sellers go invite → catalog → event + stock before the show.
  • ☐ Connectivity plan — checkout needs network (create_order RPC); expo wifi is unreliable → mobile-data/hotspot per booth. (Offline mode = too big for 9 weeks.)
  • ☐ Data safety — reopen #41 (staging + backups) by early July, before real sales depend on it.
  • ☐ Device check — confirm the app works on the sellers' actual phones/tablets.
  • ☐ Live-support plan — who fixes a booth jam in 60 seconds during the 4 days.

5 · Three de-risks (strongly recommended)

  • Be seller #0. Dogfood MochiPOS at your own Meowseum booth (cat scratchers + pet accessories) + a dry-run at a smaller event before July 30. Don't let the first real use be 5 strangers at the championship.
  • Recruit + onboard in parallel with the build — not after it.
  • Cut ruthlessly. Minimum-viable-booth = catalog · stock · fast checkout · payment · receipt (day-1 must-haves). Samples / fancy dashboard can fast-follow if time runs short.
